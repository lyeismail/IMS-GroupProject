Travel Packing Tips!

1. Roll, don't fold.

2. Make a packing list.

3. Know your airline's baggage-fee policy.

4. Follow the 3-1-1 rule.

5. Use your personal item wisely.

6. Wash your clothes on the road.

7. Pack dual-purpose garments.

8. Layer.


Safety Tips!

1. Do your research.

2. Get to know your destination in depth before you arrive.

3. Don't draw attention.

4. Make copies of important documents.

5. Keep your friends and family updated.

6. Be wary of public Wi-Fi.

7. Safeguard your hotel room.

8. Be aware of your surroundings
